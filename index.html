Diligent, dependable, addicted to success.

Quickly learning three full web development stacks (LAMP, MEAN and Ruby on Rails) testifies to my dedication to success in web development. Patient and determined, I meet my obligations, period. My primary goal is to be effective in my work, and I believe that with diligent organization and clear communication that goal can be achieved.

Before I became interested in web development I worked in Geomorphology and Environmental Compliance in federal and state government. From those experiences I have gained a unique perspective on teamwork (after working with politicians I can help a team work together smoothly), responsibility (literally being responsible for my subordinates’ lives during fieldwork), and a passion for how technology can change lives (making tools for compliance with federal/state regulations).

Coding can become an addiction – I’m hooked. Creating an application from scratch and seeing that your creation performs as intended directly stimulates the brain’s reward pathway. So, scientifically speaking, programming is genuinely fun and the ability to work in a career that I love is gratifying. I’m incredibly excited for my future in development.
